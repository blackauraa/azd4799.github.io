Arizona: One of the Best States in the US

Arizona is a very diverse state, which adds to many great features to the state. There is a wide variety of different types of geography which makes each city and town interesting. Arizona is known as the Grand Canyon State due to the beautiful nature and features up north. There are three specific cities that are very special that will be introduced here. As an Arizona Native, I have been to all three of these wonderful cities and there are many great features about them.

The three cities are:

  1. Phoenix
  2. Flagstaff
  3. Tucson
